The Future of Bhutan

Gelephu Mindfulness City (GMC) is designed to be a global hub for sustainable living. It integrates economic opportunities with our core values of Gross National Happiness.

The Vision Of Druk Gyalpo

“The purpose of establishing this SAR is to create a vibrant economic hub by providing a conducive business environment and compelling incentives. It will be a Mindfulness City, encompassing conscious and sustainable businesses, inspired by Buddhist spiritual heritage, and distinguished by the uniqueness of the Bhutanese identity.”
