The Wonders of Bhutan

Explore the breathtaking places in the kingdom of Bhutan!

Paro Taktsang

One of the places, considered one of the most sacred pilgrimage sites in the Himalayas, it is believed to be where Guru Rinpoche (Padmasambhava) introduced Buddhism to Bhutan.As the "Tiger's Nest," it carries the essence of Bhutanese culture and is a deeply significant symbol for its people, often representing the country globally.

Phobjikha Valley

A famous site for the sightings of Black-Necked Cranes. A breathtaking place best for relaxtion and vaction. The valleys are mesmerizing and beautiful.

Buddha Dordenma

A gigantic Shakyamuni Buddha statue in the mountains of Bhutan. It ranks among the largest sitting Buddha statues in the world.
